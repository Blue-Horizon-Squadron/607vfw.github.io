We're excited to announce some important updates for our community!

## Server Update 
Our 60-slot NimbusPulse DCS Server trial/testing phase is coming to an end on 01/04/26. We're grateful for the opportunity, and we're pleased to announce that on 31/03/26 we're moving to a new server with the same host. The new server will offer 40 slots—still plenty of room for our regular operations and events. As our server and community grows we will eventually upgrade to a bigger server however at this moment in time this isn't currently strategically/financially viable.

With our move however, we can officially say that we will have the ability to switch between 4 terrains, those terrains of choice will be Caucasus, Syria, Persian Gulf and Marianas/Marianas WWI. Marianas will be variable however Cold War Germany will not be an option.

## Support the Squadron  
To keep our server running smoothly, we're inviting the community to support us on [Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/bluehorizonsquadron) or Directly Via [Stripe](https://buy.stripe.com/9B6cN54eKbBb6ZXcIfaIM01) while our host works on Direct Server Donation functionality. Every contribution helps, and your support ensures we can continue providing a great experience for everyone.

## Squadron Name Change  
We've officially rebranded as **607 Virtual Fighter Wing - Blue Horizon Squadron**. This new name reflects our evolving identity and commitment to excellence in the DCS community.

## Campaign & Mission Updates
Our Black Flag campaign is being rebuilt from the ground up. As a first step, we've implemented Persistent Randomised FARPs to enhance mission variety and replayability. Next up, we're focusing on Logistics (CTLD), CSAR, and Warehouse/Supply operations to create a more immersive and dynamic experience.

Thank you for flying with us and being part of our journey. Stay tuned for more updates, and see you in the skies!
